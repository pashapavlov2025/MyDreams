## Description EN

Take control of your finances with a simple, private, and powerful approach. MyDreams is a personal finance and asset tracker built around one idea: update your balances once a month, and let the app do the rest.

No bank connections. No complicated imports. Just open the app, enter your current numbers, and watch your financial picture come into focus over time.

HOW IT WORKS

MyDreams uses a snapshot-based approach. Each month, you record your account balances — bank accounts, cash, brokerage, crypto, real estate, debts. The app builds your financial history automatically, showing your Net Worth trend, monthly changes, and asset allocation.

It takes five minutes a month. That is all you need.

TRACK EVERYTHING IN ONE PLACE

- Bank accounts, savings, and cash
- Brokerage and investment accounts
- Cryptocurrency holdings
- Real estate and property values
- Debts and liabilities
- Group accounts by bank for a clear overview

INVESTMENT PROJECT TRACKING

Got a villa in Bali? A renovation project? A startup investment? MyDreams lets you track investment projects with full P&L — tranches, construction costs, rental income, operating expenses, and sales. See your total invested, net profit, and ROI at a glance.

MULTI-CURRENCY SUPPORT

Hold assets in different currencies? No problem. MyDreams supports all major fiat currencies and cryptocurrencies with live exchange rates. Everything is automatically converted to your base currency for a unified Net Worth view.

BEAUTIFUL CHARTS & INSIGHTS

- Net Worth trend over time (area chart)
- Asset allocation breakdown (donut chart)
- Month-over-month delta with percentage changes
- Per-account balance history
- Project P&L timeline

YOUR DATA STAYS ON YOUR DEVICE

Privacy is not a feature — it is the foundation. All your financial data is stored locally on your device. There are no cloud servers, no accounts to create, no data leaving your phone. Ever. Your finances are nobody's business but yours.

PIN code protection keeps your data safe if someone picks up your device.

BUILT FOR REAL LIFE

- Multi-profile support — track personal and family finances separately
- Demo profile with sample data to explore before you start
- Works offline — no internet required after setup
- Clean, minimal interface in English and Russian
- Dark-friendly design that is easy on the eyes

MyDreams is for people who want clarity without complexity. No subscription. No ads. No data harvesting. Just a straightforward tool that helps you see where you stand financially — and where you are heading.

Start tracking today. Five minutes a month is all it takes.
